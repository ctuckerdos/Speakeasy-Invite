# 🥃 SHHH... The Speakeasy Invite

*An interactive, Prohibition-era invitation with attitude — and a touch of Marauder's Map magic.*

---

## 📖 What is this?

This is a fully interactive, **sarcastic speakeasy-style invitation** designed for a themed party or event. It's 8 pages of vintage chaos — complete with runaway buttons, password puzzles, judgmental attire options, a hidden address reveal, and **actual 🐾 footprints that scatter across your screen** when you swear an oath.

**TL;DR:** Your guests will have to earn that drink. And maybe a trip to Azkaban.

---

## 🕵️ How it works

Open `index.html` in any browser. No servers, no installs — just click and suffer (in a fun way).

### The 8-Page Flow:

| Page | Title | Vibe |
|------|-------|------|
| 1 | **The Invite** | "Will you meet us?" — clicking "No" is physically impossible. It runs away and mocks you. |
| 2 | **Arrival Time** | Pick 6:30 or 7:00. The other times? They *literally run away* from your cursor. |
| 3 | **The Password** | Marilyn Monroe trivia. Get it wrong? Get roasted. Get it right? Champagne appears. |
| 4 | **Attire Check** | Five outfit options — from "lazy sweats" to "clothes are required?" — each with a snarky response. |
| 5 | **Drink Orders** | Six Prohibition-era cocktails. Each click gets a reply. All are valid. No wrong answers (except judgment). |
| 6 | **The Real Reason** | A trap. All answers are correct — we just want to see if you have a sense of humor. |
| 7 | **Address Reveal** | Click "I SWEAR I'M NOT A RAT" to dissolve the message and reveal: **233 Broad St, Rome, GA 30161**. Then click the **Harry Potter-inspired oath** — *"I solemnly swear I am up to no good"* — to trigger 🐾 footprints across the screen. |
| 8 | **Confirmation** | A summary page showing *your* time, attire, drink, and reason. Personalized. Humiliating. Perfect. |

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Runaway buttons** — try clicking 8pm, we dare you
- ✅ **Sarcastic feedback** — every click gets a reply, including wrong ones
- ✅ **Champagne bottle reveal** — when you get the password right
- ✅ **Address dissolve effect** — because secrets shouldn't be easy
- ✅ **🐾 Footprint scatter animation** — inspired by the Marauder's Map. Say the oath and watch them track across your screen
- ✅ **Summary page** — remembers what you chose (shame included free of charge)
- ✅ **Prohibition-era bar/booth background** — wood grain, velvet texture, moody lighting — all pure CSS, no external images
- ✅ **Mobile responsive** — works on phones, so you can RSVP from the bathtub
- ✅ **Page linger** — each reply stays on screen long enough to read before moving on
